## Vocabulary Apps

- [Memrise](https://www.memrise.com/) - Learn German vocabulary with user-generated courses and spaced repetition.
- [Drops](https://languagedrops.com/) - Visual vocabulary learning with 5-minute sessions.
- [Der Die Das](https://der-die-das.com/) - Focus on German articles and noun gender.

## Grammar Apps

- [Grammatisch](https://grammatisch.de/) - Comprehensive German grammar exercises and explanations.
- [Deutsche Grammatik](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=de.deutsche_grammatik) - Grammar exercises for Android devices.

## Speaking & Pronunciation Apps

- [Speechling](https://speechling.com/deutsch) - Free German pronunciation practice with native speaker feedback.
- [ELSA Speak German](https://elsaspeak.com/) - AI-powered pronunciation training.

## Dictionary Apps

- [German-English Dictionary App](https://german-english-dictionary.devtheworld.jp/) - For scientifically-efficient modern learning.
- [Leo Dictionary](https://www.leo.org/) - Comprehensive German-English dictionary with forums.
- [Dict.cc](https://www.dict.cc/) - Extensive German dictionary with audio pronunciation.

## Comprehensive Learning Platforms

- [Duolingo](https://www.duolingo.com/) - Gamified language learning with bite-sized lessons.
- [Babbel](https://www.babbel.com/) - Conversation-focused German courses.
- [Busuu](https://www.busuu.com/) - Learn German with grammar lessons and vocabulary practice.
- [Lingoda](https://www.lingoda.com/) - Live online German classes with certified teachers.

## Reading & Listening Apps

- [LingQ](https://www.lingq.com/) - Learn German through reading and listening to content.
- [Beelinguapp](https://www.beelinguapp.com/) - Read bilingual texts side-by-side.
- [News in Slow German](https://www.newsinslowgerman.com/) - German news at slower speeds for learners.

## Flashcard Apps

- [Anki](https://apps.ankiweb.net/) - Powerful spaced repetition flashcard system.
- [Quizlet](https://quizlet.com/) - Create and study German flashcard sets.
- [Brainscape](https://www.brainscape.com/) - Adaptive flashcards for German learning.
